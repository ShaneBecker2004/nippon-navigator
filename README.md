# Nippon Navigator
- Nippon Navigator is a website application designed to help travelers explore cities in Japan and plan their travel itinerary. 
- This is a Japan Travel Itinerary Website for my Senior Capstone Project. This website will give users a user-friendly experience to help plan for future trips to Japan, whether it may be looking up best places to eat or see, or fun activities to do, and even recommend best places to stay or help you discover some hidden gems other tourists may overlook.

## Features
- Explore major Japanese cities
- Search recommended activities, hidden gems, or other kinds of activities
- View travel details like VISA and immigration policies, recommendations like apps, phrases, etc., and more useful information
- Plan your entire trip from the day you get there to the day you leave
